In the midst of the ongoing humaniarian crisis in Ukraine, there have been reports of foreign students receiving racist treatment at the border, with social media footage showing Ukrainian officials preventing some from crossing.

#AfricansinUkraine is an Exploratory Data Analysis in which scrape tweets with the hashtags #AfricansinUkraine, #AfricansinPoland, and #SaveSumyStudents to analyze the negative sentiment against non-white refugees fleeing the war in Ukraine. We are also looking into how this correlates to the lack of diversity in refugees particularly non-white refugees being accepted into neighboring countries that have agreed to accept refugees.
