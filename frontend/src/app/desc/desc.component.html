In the midst of the ongoing humaniarian crisis in Ukraine, over 2.5 million people have fled the country. However, many refugees have reported instances of racism and exclusion, flooding social media with accounts of foreign students and people of color being targeted with violence and prevented from exiting the country safely.

#AfricansinUkraine is an Exploratory Data Analysis in which scrape tweets with the hashtags #AfricansinUkraine, #AfricansinPoland, and #SaveSumyStudents to analyze the negative sentiment against non-white refugees fleeing the war in Ukraine. We are also looking into how this correlates to the lack of diversity in refugees being accepted into neighboring countries that have previously agreed to accept refugees.
